Welcome to our website. We at Somani Lodge are committed to worth each moment you spent with our hospitality and scenic environment which we have got by fortune. We are based in a region with a distinct culture where tribal dances are its golden treasure. Why choose Us

The experience of any tour depends a lot on its stay and with Somani Lodge, you get nothing but the very best. We offer the best hotel room in Jhargram that is spacious, airy, and packed with the latest amenities. Each of our rooms are testimony to the superior interior decor that is going to make your stay that good. Furnished with deluxe beds, our rooms will give you the best Jhargram experience.

Luxury

Whenever we plan a holiday, one of the major thoughts that comes into our mind is accommodation with comfort and luxury. Every room of ours is equipped with Air-Conditioner, LED Television, personal wardrobe, attached bathroom with installed geysers which will surely uplift one’s level of luxury while on board.

SIT, RELAX & ENJOYING

Jhargram is a popular tourist destination known for its forest, ancient temples and royal palaces. Somani Lodge is located in the midst of breathtaking green scenery where you can sit back, relax enjoying the experience of amazing early morning and sunset views with a cup of hot beverage and snacks.

Services

We undertake ‘Service’ section very actively because we believe providing good service is the only way to build a healthy bond with customers. Free internet access, intercom for door-step room service, laundry, Diesel Generator ( In case of Load shedding) and housekeeping are some complimentary services we offer to our guests.
